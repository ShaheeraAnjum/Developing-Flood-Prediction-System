Rising Waters

Machine Learning-Powered Flood Prediction

Rising Waters focuses on addressing the growing risk of floods, which have become increasingly frequent and severe due to climate change, rapid urbanization, and unpredictable weather patterns.

By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms like XGBoost on historical weather data, this system enables disaster management authorities and local communities to detect potential flood risks proactively, ensuring early warnings and effective resource allocation.
